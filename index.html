The World Happiness Report provides valuable insights into the happiness and well-being of nations across the globe. The data is published yearly by the world happiness report. Visualising the data from this report can help us understand the factors influencing happiness scores and identify trends over time. The top 50 countries for each indicator have been displayed according to their overall happiness score. Hover over the visuals for additional details relating to the Happinness score and metrics of each country. The data was collected from kaggle 2015-2022 and kaggle 2023.
